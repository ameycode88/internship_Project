Services

Web Design

I specialize in creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites tailored to both individual and business needs. With a focus on modern design principles and performance, I ensure every site I build is visually appealing, accessible, and optimized for various devices and screen sizes.

Android Development

From idea to deployment, I develop Android applications that are fast, reliable, and easy to use. Whether it's a simple utility app or a feature-rich mobile solution, I ensure smooth performance, intuitive interfaces, and compatibility with the latest Android versions and devices.

Media Editing

I offer professional media editing services, including video, audio, and image enhancements. Whether it's creating promotional content, refining visual storytelling, or polishing raw footage, I bring creativity and attention to detail to deliver high-quality, engaging media.

Data Analytics

Recent Projects

Project 1

EcoNest Website

The site features clean navigation, interactive content sections, and a custom CMS for blog and product management. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and integrated with a lightweight backend.

Project 2

TaskTrail App

Created TaskTrail, a productivity Android app that helps users organize their daily routines through to-do lists, reminders, and calendar integration. The app features a sleek UI, offline functionality, and local storage using Room Database. Built with Kotlin and Jetpack components.

Project 3

ReelFusion Showreel

Produced and edited a high-energy promotional showreel titled ReelFusion for a local startup’s social media campaign. The project involved data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and visualizations using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) and Power BI. The insights helped the client optimize inventory and identify peak sales periods.
